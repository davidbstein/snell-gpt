This webpage visualizes the possible responses GPT-3.5 might have generated in response to the questions Judge Newsom experiments with in his concurrance in Snell v. United Ins. Co. (11th Cir. 2024)

Each box shows a piece of text that GPT-3.5 might have generated. The probability of GPT-3.5 picking that box is shown to the left of the text. On the bottom of each box is the probability of GPT-3.5 saying that in-ground trampolines fall within the definition of the word "landscaping," given that it has chosen the answer in that box.

Clicking a box will show you the next set of words considered by GPT-3.5. Pressing the "+" key on your keyboard will expand every box on the screen.
